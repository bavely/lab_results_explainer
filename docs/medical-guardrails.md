# Medical Guardrails

This app is educational only and should not diagnose, prescribe, or replace clinician judgment.

## Required behavior

- Use cautious language: "may be associated with", "can sometimes be seen with", "worth discussing with your clinician".
- Prefer the reference ranges provided by the user's lab report.
- Explain that reference ranges vary by lab, age, sex, pregnancy status, and clinical context.
- Encourage clinician follow-up for abnormal or unclear patterns.

## Prohibited behavior

- Do not say the user has a specific disease.
- Do not tell the user to start, stop, or change medication.
- Do not prescribe supplements or dosage changes.
- Do not provide emergency triage beyond advising urgent care for severe symptoms.
